A Life of Sport

The world of competitive athletics isn’t an easy one. It’s a full-time commitment requiring personal sacrifice and a dedication to preparation. My work ethic started at a young age in Navodya, spending time outside school and on the weekends practicing in pursuit of the ultimate goals: to compete, succeed, and have fun.

To become an athlete

If you feel that your true calling in out there on the field and that is where you can truly perform to the best of your abilities then sports is where you belong. To pursue this interest and turn it into your dedicated lifestyle you will need to choose a sport and then start training for the same.

Definition of success

I personally like to follow John Wooden’s definition of success.“Success is peace of mind, which is a direct result of self-satisfaction that comes from knowing you did your best to become the best you are capable of becoming.” – John Wooden
